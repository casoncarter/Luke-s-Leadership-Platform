Module 1

The Secret Power of the Dad Joke

Why Groan-Inducing Humor Actually Works

Core Concepts

Disarms tension — A pun is a “soft landing” for any conversation.

Signals approachability — It shows you don’t take yourself too seriously.

Creates a shared moment — People bond through the little eye-roll + smile combo.

Builds confidence — Delivering a joke, even a silly one, trains timing and voice.

Teaches communication — Dad jokes force clarity: setup → twist → laugh.

Mini Case Study — Coach K’s Dad Joke Play Even Coach K would open team meetings with light humor. Not puns, but the same principle: gentle levity creates trust. Dad jokes operate on that same frequency.

Module 2

The Dad Joke Blueprint

The Anatomy of a Perfect (Terrible) Joke

A classic dad joke has three parts:

The Setup (Serious Face Engaged) A normal-sounding question or statement.

The Misdirection (The Audience Leans In) They think you’re going one direction.

The Twist (Certified Groan Activation) A pun, unexpected meaning, or absurd logic.

Formula:

Setup → Expectation → Twist That Makes No Sense but Still Makes Sense

Example: “Why don’t eggs tell jokes?” Because they’d crack each other up.

Module 3

The Five Sacred Dad Joke Archetypes

1. The Classic Pun

Wordplay that gently hurts the brain.

“I’m reading a book on anti-gravity. It’s impossible to put down.”

2. The Question/Answer Groaner

“Why did the scarecrow win an award?” He was outstanding in his field.

3. The Literal Interpretation Joke

“My boss told me to have a good day… so I went home.”

4. The Overly-Confident Misunderstanding

“I used to play piano by ear… now I use my hands.”

5. The Unexpected Wisdom Joke

Surprisingly profound… but still dumb.

“Never trust atoms. They make up everything.”

Module 4

How to Deliver Dad Jokes Like a Pro

Timing, Expression, and Maximum Groan Factor

1. Say it completely deadpan Like you genuinely think this joke is brilliant.

2. Hold eye contact for 0.5 seconds after the punchline Allows the joke to land (or crash).

3. Accept both laughter and groans as victory The groan is the applause.

4. Use the environment See objects around you and create on-the-fly puns.

Example: At dinner, waiter asks, “How’s the food?” You answer:

“It’s pasta-tively amazing.”

Module 5

Write Your Own Dad Jokes

A Practical Framework

Step 1 — Pick a normal object (e.g., stairs, pencils, clouds, shoes)

Step 2 — List verbs or ideas associated with it Stairs: climb, fall, step, elevate, up/down.

Step 3 — Find double meanings “Step up,” “falling down,” “high vs. low.”

Step 4 — Combine into a twist Example creation process:

Object: stairs

Associated idea: they’re always up to something

Joke: “Why don’t you trust stairs? They’re always up to something.”

Boom — instant dad joke.

CLOSING MESSAGE FOR LUKE

Luke, Mastering dad jokes is less about puns and more about perspective. It means being lighthearted, quick-witted, observant, and brave enough to say something goofy even in serious rooms. Humor is a quiet form of leadership — it warms the room, eases tension, and reminds people to breathe.

If you can hold wisdom in one hand and ridiculousness in the other, you’ll be a rare and joyful presence wherever you go.

And just remember… If anyone ever asks if you’re serious about dad jokes, you can say:

“No… I’m Sirius. The brightest star in the dad-joke galaxy.”
