Executive Summary

Dad jokes are more than simple puns — they are a way of life. They are a leadership style, a communication strategy, an emotional icebreaker, and a secret superpower that makes awkward moments magically less awkward.

  • They lower defenses and make people feel safe.
  • They create shared moments (eye-roll + smile = bonding).
  • They build confidence in your voice, timing, and presence.
  • They train structure: setup → misdirection → twist.

MODULE 1 — The Secret Power of the Dad Joke

Why groan-inducing humor actually works

Core concepts

  • Disarms tension — A pun is a “soft landing” for any conversation.
  • Signals approachability — It shows you don’t take yourself too seriously.
  • Creates a shared moment — People bond through the little eye-roll + smile combo.
  • Builds confidence — Delivering a joke trains timing and voice.
  • Teaches communication — Dad jokes force clarity: setup → twist → laugh.

Leadership insight: Great leaders often begin with levity to lower defenses. Dad jokes are the playful version of the same strategy.
